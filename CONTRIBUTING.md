# Contributing

This repository accepts contributions through pull requests.

## CLA Requirement

By submitting a contribution, you agree that your contribution is subject to the Neuralchowder Contributor License Agreement (CLA).

- Individual contributors must accept the ICLA.
- Contributors acting for a company must ensure a CCLA is in place.

Maintainers should ensure CLA status is satisfied before merge.

## Sign-offs

A `Signed-off-by` line is welcome but does not replace CLA requirements.
